EREN YEAGER

Eren Yeager (エレン・イェーガー Eren Yēgā?) was a former member of the Survey Corps. He was the main protagonist of Attack on Titan. He lived in Shiganshina District with his parents until the fall of Wall Maria, where he impotently witnessed his mother being eaten by a Titan.This event would lead to Eren's intense hatred towards the Titans as he swore to wipe all of them off the face of the Earth

Soon afterward, his father, Grisha Yeager, found him and gave him the key to his basement, instructing Eren to find it at all costs and retake Wall Maria.He then injected Eren with a Titan serum.In the year 847, Eren, Mikasa Ackerman, and Armin Arlert joined the 104th Training Corps. The three graduated with Eren ranking 5th best,and they joined the Survey Corps following the battle of Trost District.

After finding the basement and unlocking his father's memories, Eren learned about the truth of what the Titans are, as well as the history of Eldia and Marley. Eren then vowed to free his home from their true enemy: the rest of humanity that lives across the ocean.Eren possessed the power of three Titans. From his father, Eren inherited the Attack and Founding Titans.After eating Lara Tybur during the Raid on Liberio, he gained the War Hammer Titan as well.

BIOGRAPHY

Eren starts as a young boy living within the walled city of Shiganshina, a world surrounded by enormous walls to protect humanity from giant humanoid creatures known as Titans. His life takes a tragic turn when a colossal Titan breaches the walls, leading to the loss of his mother and his determination to seek revenge on the Titans.

Throughout the series, Eren's character evolves from a vengeful, impulsive teenager to a complex leader. He joins the Scout Regiment, where he demonstrates exceptional combat skills and a relentless pursuit of freedom for humanity. Eren possesses a unique ability to transform into a Titan himself, known as the Attack Titan..

As the series progresses, Eren's motivations become more nuanced. His desire for freedom from the Titans expands into a broader ambition to free humanity from the oppressive Marleyan rule and uncover the mysteries of the world. Eren's character grapples with moral dilemmas, and his actions spark a global conflict..

Eren's journey is marked by both heroism and controversy. His character represents the complexities of human nature, the consequences of one's choices, and the pursuit of freedom and truth in a harsh world filled with uncertainty..

Overall, Eren Yeager's biography is a tale of transformation, determination, and the moral gray areas that shape his character in the world of "Attack on Titan." It's a story that resonates with themes of freedom, sacrifice, and the human spirit.
